# Privacy Policy — Vocabularify

_Last updated: 2026-06-17_

Vocabularify ("the app") is designed to respect your privacy completely.

## Data we collect

**None.** The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to us or to any third party. It has no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking.

## Data stored on your device

The app saves your preferences **locally on your device only**, using the operating system's standard local storage:

- the language pair and level you selected,
- your learning mode and word-change frequency,
- the index of the last flashcard you viewed,
- your theme preference (light / dark / system).

This information never leaves your device. Uninstalling the app removes it.

## Network access

The app works fully offline. The only time it accesses the internet is when **you tap an external link** (for example the author or donation links), which opens that page in your device's browser. We do not control or receive data from those external sites.

## Children's privacy

Because the app collects no data, it is safe for users of all ages.

## Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this location with a new "last updated" date.
